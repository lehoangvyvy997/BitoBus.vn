[IT] HCM - Product QC/ Tester ( Fresher)

As a Product QC / Tester, you will ensure the quality of development product meet the standard within given time.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Working with product owner to clarify requirements and what need to be tested.

  • Preparing testing plan, including test cases and checklist.

  • Execute testing activities, for both general and exploratory approach.

  • Report and follow fixing activity, to control the product quality before deadline.

  • Be responsible for product quality at all time.

Requirements:

  • Reading and writing in English is a must.

  • Experience with testing web/mobile applications is a plus.

  • Very detail and strong responsibility.

  • Logical thinking and trustworthy are required.

Benefits

  • Attractive salary

  • Insurance according to Vietnamese Labor Law

  • Special discount policies for VeXeRe members and their family/relatives

  • Paid annual leaves

  • Company trips, Team-building, Year-end party, and other festivities

  • Entrepreneurial environment with young and ambitious colleagues

  • Opportunities to work closely with founders and learn how to grow a startup

  • Opportunities to take initiatives, make an impact and become a true leader

  • Other benefits are further discussed at the final interview
