[HCM] - Top React Native/mobile developers wanted

We are moving to District 1 by the end of this year 2018 to welcome all talents. Be one of them!
 
“For, in the end, it is impossible to have a great life unless it is a meaningful life. And it is very difficult to have a meaningful life without meaningful work.” – James C. Collins
“For millions of Vietnamese people never have to queue to buy ticket…again” – Van Tran – CEO
 
Join us to be part of a warrior team to conquer any difficulty, to strive for the best user experience. You will be part of the team to build all components in VeXeRe design language system and put to storybook where other teams can come and use your components at easy. Part of mobile team, you will build fast and sleek mobile apps in React Native. You are giving the chance to build mobile apps used by millions of users.
We are proud to provide to you many challenging works while solving society problems, to make your life and your family’s life much better.
 
Benefits
  • Company will plan and help you to build publish profile by publishing posts in medium.com anh vexere techblog under your name, being contributor in opensource projects managed by our company, tech talks
  • Many chances to lead new and challenging products as we are going fast like a Lamborghini[D1] [LL2] .
  • Attractive stock option for hard-core developer/lead
  • Opportunities to get mentor from top CTOs from other startups
  • Salary:
    • Junior 700$ and above, Senior: up to 3.000
    • Attractive bonus
  • Part of a great team that set their product standard to be at global level. Our mindset is always learning from top companies in the field such as Airbnb, redbus, booking, agoda…[D3] [LL4] 
  • 30% discount for VeXeRe members and 5% for their family/relatives when buying bus tickets to travel within Vietnam
  • Company trips, Teambuilding, Year-end party, and other festivities

Responsibilities
  • Being a part of a Product team, your work will be solving business requirements by designing solutions and shipping codes with quality.
  • Being a member of the Mobile Chapter, you will be proactively analyzing things like technical debts and challenges to be improved and making changes. Learn and share within Mobile Chapter to maximize knowledge of your team as well as yours.
  • Lead mobile team or a product if capable.
 Requirements
  • At least one year of real work experience with React Native, must understand basic philosophy of React Native and React component lifecycle
  • Solid understanding of how to convert design into mobile application, ability to effectively layout to adapt to different screen sizes
  • Experience with caching, memory management, concurrency (thread, queue), Restful API, Push notification
  • Good understanding of data structures, algorithms and design patterns
  • Being serious about code quality, experience in doing code review, familiar with unit testing
  • Grow mindset: as our company grows 3 to 5 times a year, as a member you have no choice but to grow at least 3 times a year
 Preferred
  • Having your own apps and able to show us how awesome it is
  • Having public profiles at GitHub/IT blogs
  • Having user-centric mind
  • Attention to detail
