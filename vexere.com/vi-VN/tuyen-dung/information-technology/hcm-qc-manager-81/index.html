HCM - QC Manager

VeXeRe is a technology company aiming to revolutionize the tourism and transportation industry in Vietnam. Today VeXeRe is the largest online bus ticketing platform that empowering million travelers to bring more happiness to their journey. At VeXeRe, QC Manager role is at the heart of our company's growth. You will be given an exclusive opportunity to make a positive impact on millions of customers.

Job decription:
As a QC Manager, you will be responsible for ensuring the quality of our software & Apps & Web products and performing the testing tasks as following:
Manage 4 QC Engineers (doing both manual and automation testing) who work in multiple Scrum teams 
Implement standardized quality management process software, coaching, training QC members and motivate them
Designing and write a test strategy, test cases, test procedure to ensure stability, good security software. 
Organize the review of test plans and test cases of Scrum teams
Performing functional test at the level of all new features based on the test case designed 
Analyzing test results and posting bugs. 
Develop the QC team members in both technical and soft skills
Work closely with Engineering Managers, Product Managers and Scrum Masters to ensure the delivery of Scrum teams
Support the recruitment of QC Engineers 

Requirements:
Minimum 5 years of experience in software testing
Excellent leadership skills with at least 3 years of hands-on experience in people management 
Experience in Test Automation, API Testing, Performance Testing, Mobile Testing, Security Testing, Penetration Testing are huge advantages.
Experience in Scrum is a plus

Benefits:
Basic salary up to 30mil
Insurance according to Vietnamese Labor Law
Special discount policies for VeXeRe members and their family/relatives
Paid annual leaves
Company trips, Teambuilding, Year-end party, and other festivities
Entrepreneurial environment with young and ambitious colleagues
Opportunities to work closely with founders and learn how to grow a business
Other benefits are further discussed at the final interview

Location: 83/16 Thoai Ngoc Hau, Hoa Thanh, Tan Phu, HCM

About VeXeRe:
VeXeRe is the largest long-distance bus ticket platform in Vietnam, helping customers search information and buy long-distance bus tickets online. VeXeRe is selling tickets for more than 120 bus companies in Vietnam, covering all major routes in Vietnam. VeXeRe is also the leader in providing Bus Management Software which is used by 200 bus companies, contributing to the modernization of the long-distance bus industry.
We believe an organization should be born to solve critical issues of the society. Our culture emphasizes teamwork and community values. All VeXeRe members are given opportunities to lead with initiatives on products and strategy, and are expected to become future leaders.
