[HCM] - CEO Assistant

The responsibility of the assistant is to provide support and office coordination to the organization while ensuring the installation of appropriate systems in order to achieve team success. Moreover, this position requires candidates to control the information and calendars of all the appointments and act as the Hand of CEO to manage the organization effectively
 

The best summary for this position is: “You will do whatever I do” - said Mr. Van Tran, Founder and CEO of Vexere.com


Qualifications

  • Strong organizational skills that reflect ability to perform and prioritize multiple tasks seamlessly with excellent attention to detail
  • Strong Interpersonal and relationship-building skills
  • Excellent in both verbal and written communication skills
  • Demonstrated proactive approaches to problem-solving with strong decision-making capability
  • Emotional maturity
  • Highly resourceful team-player, with the ability to also be extremely effective independently
  • Result-oriented and ability to work under pressure.
  • Forward looking thinker, who actively seeks opportunities and proposes solutions.
  • Understanding of all aspects of a business e.g. finance, marketing, sales, research, product dev/IT (IT background is a plus)
  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Computer Science, Information Management or Finance from top local or overseas universities with min GPA 3.5 on a 4.0 scale or 8.0 on a 10.0, MBA / Master's / PhD is a plus

Location: 83/16 Thoai Ngoc Hau, Hoa Thanh, Tan Phu, HCM

Salary: Negotiable

How to apply: Pls send your CV to tuyendung@vexere.com together with your assignment:

  • What do you want to do with your life? (What are the dreams and purposes of your life?)
  • Do you think that you more special and deserve to choose than other candidates? And Why?
  • Make a self-judgement about your persornallities, your strong points, your weakness and your interests, also your health. (about 100-150 words)
  • Present achievements you most proud of. (including your study, your jobs, your life, your social activities,..)
  • Your big failure in past. (including your study, your jobs, your life, your social activities,..)
  • In your opinion, if you get involved in VeXere, how will VeXere contribute your plan in life?

 

About VeXeRe:

VeXeRe is the largest long-distance bus ticket platform in Vietnam, helping customers search information and buy long-distance bus tickets online. VeXeRe is selling tickets for more than 120 bus companies in Vietnam, covering all major routes in Vietnam. VeXeRe is also the leader in providing Bus Management Software which is used by 200 bus companies, contributing to the modernization of the long-distance bus industry.

We believe an organization should be born to solve critical issues of the society. Our culture emphasizes teamwork and community values. All VeXeRe members are given opportunities to lead with initiatives on products and strategy, and are expected to become future leaders.

VeXeRe was invested by CyberAgent Ventures (Japan) & Pix Vine Capital (Singapore).
