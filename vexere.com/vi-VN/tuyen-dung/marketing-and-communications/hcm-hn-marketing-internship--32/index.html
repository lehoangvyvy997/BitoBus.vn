[HCM - HN] Marketing Internship

The chosen Intern will be responsible for assisting the Marketing team in the development and implementation of marketing strategy, business development, and public relation plans. This will be accomplished through developing, maintaining and expanding marketing channels.
 
Primary Responsibilities
  • Support in developing and delivering the vision, mission and gross profit for VeXeRe.com brand
  • Manage the day-to-day interface and key business activities of the portfolio 
  • Assume accountability and autonomy to lead cross-functional teams in the development and execution of strategies for advertising, packaging, promotions, and operations
  • Support to develop, recommend and lead the execution of marketing plans for assigned brands and manage the effective deployment of the marketing budget
  • Develop and analyze promotion programs
  • Develop, recommend and execute annual marketing plan activities, including local media strategy and plans
  • Communicate cross-functionally to understand business needs
  • Complete a final project based on some or all of the above responsibilities 
 
Qualifications
  • Must have good organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to prioritize in a changing environment
  • Must have excellent interpersonal skills and follow-up skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook required
  • Ability to learn other software programs
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills required
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 
Why You'll Love Working Here
  • Allowance based on performance
  • Special discount policies for VeXeRe members and their family/relatives
  • Company trips, Teambuilding, Year-end party, and other festivities
  • Entrepreneurial environment with young and ambitious colleagues
  • Opportunities to work closely with founders and learn how to grow a startup
  • Opportunities to take initiatives, make an impact and become a true leader
  • Other benefits are further discussed at the final interview
